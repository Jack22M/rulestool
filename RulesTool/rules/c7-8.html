Playing the Game - Chapters 7 - 8



Summary Notes

Chapter 7

  • The ball has to pass fully over the line to be out of play.
  • Play is restarted by the team that did not touch the ball last.
  • If the ball goes over the backline and was last touched by a defender, a '16' restarts play being taken by a defender. A '16' is taken inline with where the ball went out and anywhere upto 15 metres from the baseline.

Normally, defenders will take the '16' fifteen metres from the baseline which is inline with the top of the circle. See image below.

  • If the ball unintentionally comes of the defenders stick last and goes over the baseline, a 'long corner' is taken on the 23 (see image below) inline with where the ball went out.
  • Rules for taking a free hit apply for the above.
  • If the ball is intentionally pushed over the baseline by a defender, a penalty corner is awarded against them.

Chapter 8

  • A goal is scored when the ball is touched inside the circle (and the ball has not left since) and goes in the goal.

    • It may touch a defenders stick or body after being touched by an attacker before going in the goal as long as it stays in the circle.

Restarting Play with a Sixteen

Play is restarted with a sixteen if the ball goes over the baseline and was last tochued by an attacker. A sixteen cam be taken anywhere inline with where it went out from the basline to the top of the circle (15 metres from the basline). In the diagram below, the green/yellow line indicates the highest that a sixteen can be taken. The red shaded area shows the that it can be taken from the basline to the fifteen metres. Note: A ball cannot be taken anywhere in the redshaded area. See examples below.


Example of Restarting Play with a Sixteen

In the following diagram, the ball goes over the baseline at A and was last touched by an attacker so therefore a sixteen restarts play. The defenders can take the ball anywhere inline with A (where it went out) and 15 metres from the basline as shown by the arrow going from A to B. Usually, most defenders will take the ball at B (inline with the top of the circle) however in some scenarios it makes sense to take it somewhere inbetween A and B.


Restarting Play with a Long Corner

Play is restarted with a long corner if the ball goes over the baseline and was last touched by a defender unintentionally. The play is restarted by an attacker on the 23 inline with where it went out. See example below for more understanding.
