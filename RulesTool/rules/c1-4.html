HockeyRules - Rules



Playing the Game - Chapters 1 - 4



Summary Notes

Chapter 1

  • This chapter briefly covers specfications about field markings and also information about dimensions of the ground.

Most of this information is not usually required for an umpire on a daily basis, and is probably directed more towards assoications and ground-makers, however it is still important for an umpire to read.

Chapter 2

  • Maximum 11 players to per each team on the field at any one point
  • Either a fully kitted goalkeeper or 11 field player

    • Kicking-backs are no longer permitted.

  • Unlimited substitution is allowed for all players and is to take place within 3 metres of the centre line. Substitution is not permitted during a short-corner.
  • Time is only stopped for goalkeeper substitutions.

Chapter 3

  • One captain per each team
  • Captains are responsible for the behaviour of their team

    • A personal penalty is awarded if a captain does not exercise the above responsibilities. A personal penalty may also be awarded to the captain if behaviour of his/her team is unaccecptable.

Chapter 4

  • This chapter covers information about players clothing and other equipment.

    • Once again most of this information is required for an umpire on a daily basis, however is still highly important for an umpire to read.

  • The ball must be where possible white.
